Overview

I'm going to talk about Continuous Integration, by that I really mean build automation. It's a broad area, and there's likely a very wide spread of experience with it in the room, so I'll go quickly and hopefully there will be something juicy in here for all of you! I'll quickly go over what CI is, why you might want to do it, and which tech you should go with so you don't waste your time re-researching it. Then I'll get onto a bunch of tidbits I've picked up specifically while setting up a Unity project under CI, since that's what we're all here for!
